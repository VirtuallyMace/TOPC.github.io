About TOP-C

TOP-C — The Other Playing Cards — exists to deliver fun, strategy, and Scripture-rooted learning in an accessible card-game format. From the New and Old Testament decks to the Junior Version, our portfolio is purpose-built for families, youth groups, and game nights.

Our design ethos is simple: clear rules, high replay value, and content fidelity. With 16+ original games and CHS-aligned structure, we empower players to be “In The Know.”
